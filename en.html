Standard FA Flow

Six standard tracking nodes for every project

Each new project can start from these six nodes to track current status, key output, owner and next action date.

01

Intake Screening

Record source, core ask, contact person and project background before deciding whether to enter FA tracking.

02

NDA Materials

Complete confidentiality arrangements first, then receive business plan, financial, equity, operating and asset materials.

03

Diligence Scope

Run preliminary or site diligence, assess feasibility and define service scope, fee model and success standards.

04

FA Signing

Confirm service content, fee structure, success fee, project period, responsibilities and exit mechanism.

05

Execution Matching

Refine materials, match investors, listed companies, industrial partners or professionals, arrange meetings and follow feedback.

06

Closing Settlement

Track terms, documents, payment, closing and success-fee settlement, then archive and review the project.
